Four pillars on every engagement.

Visibility

Token-level cost data ingested from every provider. Reconciled hourly. Filterable by provider, model, feature, team, customer.

  • OpenAI · Anthropic · Bedrock · Gemini · Azure · Groq
  • Hourly ingestion · <5 min reconciliation lag
  • Read-only by default

Attribution

Every token mapped to a feature, team, and customer cohort. Monthly chargeback exported to your finance system.

  • Custom dimensions: feature, team, tier, region
  • Cohort spend · customer-tier P&L
  • NetSuite · QuickBooks · CSV · API

Anomaly detection

Real-time alerts when spend deviates from rolling baseline. Slack, PagerDuty, email — and auto-throttle when it matters.

  • Per-feature baselines · seasonality-aware
  • Slack · PagerDuty · email · webhook

Model routing, semantic caching, and prompt compression deployed behind feature flags. A/B tested 7 days minimum.

  • Per-request quality classification
  • Semantic cache · sub-10ms hit latency

06 FAQ

Common questions.

Missing something? Email hello@finopsllm.com — we respond within one business day.

What is FinOps for LLM?
FinOps for LLM brings the FinOps Foundation framework — visibility, attribution, optimization, and accountability — to LLM spend. It includes per-feature chargeback, anomaly detection, budget governance, and continuous optimization of routing, caching, and prompts.
What does FinOps LLM do?
FinOps LLM is the platform purpose-built for LLM cost intelligence. We provide real-time spend attribution across OpenAI, Anthropic, Bedrock, and Gemini, plus automated optimization and monthly reconciliation against provider invoices.
How is pricing structured?
Two models. Performance — free audit, then 15–25% of verified monthly savings. Platform — flat tiers from $1,500/month for self-serve attribution. Most start on Performance.
How much can we save?
Typical reduction in the first full billing cycle after implementation is 38–68%, depending on architecture and traffic mix.
Which providers are supported?
OpenAI, Anthropic, Gemini, Vertex AI, AWS Bedrock, Azure OpenAI, Groq, Together, Mistral, Cohere, Fireworks, Replicate, and most open-source endpoints.
Does FinOps LLM support chargeback?
Yes. Token-level attribution to providers, models, features, teams, and customer cohorts. Monthly chargeback exported to NetSuite, QuickBooks, CSV, or API.
How do you handle customer data?
Read-only by default. Billing and usage data is enough for most attribution. Prompt data only accessed with explicit approval, for specific optimizations. NDA and DPA available on request.
How long does implementation take?
Attribution and dashboards go live in under a week. Optimization takes 3–5 weeks; savings appear on the first full provider invoice after go-live.
