Glossary

AI FinOps
Applying visibility, attribution, optimization, and accountability practices to AI and LLM spend.
Chargeback
Assigning spend to teams, products, or customers for accounting or budget ownership.
Showback
Reporting spend ownership without directly billing the owning team.
Model routing
Sending each request to the cheapest model or provider that satisfies quality and latency constraints.
Semantic caching
Reusing responses for semantically similar requests after applying a quality threshold.
