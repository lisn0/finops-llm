FinOps for LLM: a practical framework

Classic cloud FinOps starts with visibility, attribution, optimization, and accountability. LLM spend needs the same discipline, but the unit economics are different: tokens, cache reads, retries, latency classes, model quality, and provider-specific billing buckets.

1. Visibility

Normalize invoices and gateway logs into comparable usage records. Separate input, output, cache write, cache read, batch, and retry spend.

2. Attribution

Assign spend to product surfaces, teams, customers, and workloads. Without attribution, every optimization discussion becomes a generic provider-price argument.

3. Optimization

Route by quality requirement, cache stable workloads, batch offline jobs, compress repeated context, and track savings against a locked baseline.

4. Accountability

Use showback first, then chargeback once teams trust the numbers.
