LLM cost anomaly detection

LLM cost anomalies are often invisible until the monthly invoice because traffic volume, token length, retries, and model routing can all shift independently.

Signals to monitor

  • Spend per endpoint per hour
  • Tokens per request by percentile
  • Retry and fallback rate
  • Cache hit rate and cache-read token share
  • Model mix changes after deploys

Response pattern

Alert the owner, attach the deployment window, show the driver, and provide a rollback or routing-policy change. A useful alert explains what changed, not just that spend increased.
