LLM cost attribution

LLM invoices usually arrive provider-first: model, token class, region, and time window. Finance needs ownership-first: product, team, customer, feature, and business outcome.

Minimum attribution dimensions

  • Provider and model
  • Environment and endpoint
  • Team or product owner
  • Customer or tenant where contractually allowed
  • Workload class: realtime, batch, eval, enrichment, support, agent workflow

Common mistake

Do not allocate spend only by request count. A small number of long-context or reasoning-heavy calls can dominate the invoice.
