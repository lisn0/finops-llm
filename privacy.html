Privacy

FinOps LLM collects information you choose to send by email or through booking flows, plus basic site analytics.

We use Microsoft Clarity to understand how visitors use this site through behavioral metrics, heatmaps, and session replay. Clarity sets cookies and captures usage data (such as clicks, scrolling, and pages viewed); by default it masks sensitive content such as form inputs. This data is processed by Microsoft in accordance with the Microsoft Privacy Statement. To opt out, you can use your browser's "Do Not Track" / global privacy control settings or block cookies.

For audit work, the default scope is read-only billing and usage metadata. Prompt and output content is not required for baseline attribution and is only reviewed with explicit approval for a specific optimization.

To request access, correction, export, or deletion, email hello@finopsllm.com.
