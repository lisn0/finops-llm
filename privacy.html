Privacy

FinOps LLM collects information you choose to send by email or through booking flows, plus basic site analytics if enabled by the hosting environment.

For audit work, the default scope is read-only billing and usage metadata. Prompt and output content is not required for baseline attribution and is only reviewed with explicit approval for a specific optimization.

To request access, correction, export, or deletion, email hello@finopsllm.com.
